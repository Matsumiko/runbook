# Panel Density And Responsive Collapse

Filters are easy to overcrowd when the result surface is already dense.

## Density guidance

- Keep top filters closest to the results they affect.
- Group advanced filters rather than making every option first-class.
- Avoid filter chrome that dominates the page more than the data.

## Responsive collapse

- Bars can collapse into sheets, drawers, or segmented rows on narrow screens.
- Preserve active-filter visibility even when controls collapse.
- Keep apply or reset controls reachable after collapse.
