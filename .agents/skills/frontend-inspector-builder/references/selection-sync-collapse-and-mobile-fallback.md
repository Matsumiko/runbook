# Selection Sync, Collapse, And Mobile Fallback

- Keep inspector content synchronized with selection without surprising jumps.
- Make collapsed sections, pinned state, or hidden inspector state explicit when they exist.
- Preserve user orientation when the inspected object disappears or becomes unavailable.
- On smaller screens, move inspectors into drawers, sheets, or route-level detail views.
- Keep close, back, or unpin behavior obvious after the layout changes.
