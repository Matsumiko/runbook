# Zoom Density And Mobile Fallback

Gantt views are fragile under narrow screens and compressed scales.

## Zoom and scale

- Choose defaults that make the primary planning horizon obvious.
- Keep scale switches predictable and useful.

## Density

- Preserve label readability before adding more rows or bars.
- Sticky label regions or scroll affordances should be obvious when the view is wide.

## Mobile fallback

- Full gantt grids often need simplified summary, milestone, or list fallback on smaller screens.
- Preserve planning meaning after collapsing the horizontal surface.
