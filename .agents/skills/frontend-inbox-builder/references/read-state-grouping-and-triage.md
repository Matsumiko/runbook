# Read State, Grouping, And Triage

- Use unread treatment to clarify attention, not to decorate.
- Group by sections, dates, teams, or priority only when those groupings help triage.
- Keep preview snippets short enough to scan quickly.
- Surface quick actions like mark read, archive, assign, snooze, or open only when they fit the workflow.
- Preserve pinned, muted, or archived semantics when those states exist.
