# Preview Detail And Mobile Fallback

- Keep preview rows lighter than full detail context.
- If a persistent detail pane exists, ensure selection and opened detail remain synchronized.
- Distinguish unread-preview surfaces from notification-only lists or SLA queues.
- On smaller screens, switch to drill-down routes, stacked detail, or sheets rather than squeezing dense two-pane layouts.
- Preserve clear back or close behavior when leaving detail on mobile.
