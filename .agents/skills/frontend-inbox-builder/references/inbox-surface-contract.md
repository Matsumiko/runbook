# Inbox Surface Contract

- Define what the inbox contains before picking density or layout.
- Make item identity, source, unread state, and timestamp readable in one scan.
- Decide whether selection opens inline detail, a side panel, or a full route.
- Clarify which sections or tabs matter: unread, all, pinned, assigned, snoozed, archived, or similar.
- Distinguish empty inbox, filtered-empty, no-selection, and loading states.
