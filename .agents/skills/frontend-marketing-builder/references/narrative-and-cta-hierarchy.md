# Narrative And CTA Hierarchy

Build marketing pages around a clear narrative sequence.

Prefer a flow like:

1. headline and subheadline
2. primary CTA
3. proof or benefit sections
4. deeper explanation, pricing, or comparison
5. final CTA with reduced friction

Keep rules like these in mind:

- do not create multiple competing primary CTAs
- let one section answer one objection or promise at a time
- keep dense copy readable and scannable
- make the strongest business message visible before decorative extras
