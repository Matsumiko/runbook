# Proof Pricing And Conversion States

Treat proof and conversion states as product behavior, not decoration.

Account for cases like:

- pricing not finalized
- waitlist instead of instant signup
- feature not available on some plans
- form submission pending or failed
- testimonials, logos, or metrics missing finalized content

Checklist:

- proof feels credible and not generic filler
- pricing blocks make plan differences obvious
- CTA feedback is explicit
- unavailable offers or beta states are stated honestly
- mobile stacking preserves the primary CTA path
