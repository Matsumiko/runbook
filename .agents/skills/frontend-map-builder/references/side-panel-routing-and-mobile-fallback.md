# Side Panel Routing And Mobile Fallback

Maps often need a companion surface for detail and action.

## Side panel or list

- Keep detail cards or list rows visually aligned with the map's selection state.
- The panel should support the map, not compete with it.

## Route previews

- When routes are shown, make start, end, and path meaning explicit.
- Keep route controls subordinate to the main spatial job.

## Mobile fallback

- Map-first layouts may need list-first, sheet, or split fallback on smaller screens.
- Preserve spatial orientation after the layout collapses.
