# Evidence, Decision, And Rationale

- Show the evidence needed for the decision near the decision controls.
- Keep rationale fields or blocking comments close to the action they justify.
- Separate routine approvals from destructive or high-risk decisions.
- Use warnings and confirmations when the decision is hard to undo.
- If diffs or structured comparisons are central, consider handing that portion to `frontend-diff-viewer-builder`.
