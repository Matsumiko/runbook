# Comments, Escalation, And Mobile Fallback

- Distinguish informational comments from blocking review notes.
- Make escalation, defer, or request-changes paths explicit when they exist.
- Preserve review context if users switch between evidence, comments, and the summary.
- On smaller screens, stack evidence and actions or move the panel into a sheet or dedicated route.
- Keep decision buttons reachable without forcing users to scroll back through the full artifact repeatedly.
