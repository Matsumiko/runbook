# Context, Anchors, And Mobile Fallback

- Preserve enough unchanged context for orientation around changed regions.
- Keep deep links, jump-to-change controls, or sticky anchors readable when present.
- Distinguish no changes from redacted or unavailable comparison data.
- On smaller screens, prefer unified mode, stacked cards, or section-by-section comparison over cramped side-by-side columns.
- Keep scroll ownership and overflow understandable when diffs are wide or long.
