# Navigation Vs Segmentation

Choose the right switching pattern:

- tabs: related subviews or content groups where the active section matters over time
- segmented control: small, local, mutually exclusive view or mode switch
- sticky tab bar: only when orientation across long content actually benefits from persistent section switching

Guidelines:

- keep labels short and differentiable
- avoid mixing route navigation, filter chips, and tab semantics in one control
- do not overload tabs with too many actions or secondary controls
