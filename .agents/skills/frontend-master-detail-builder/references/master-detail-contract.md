# Master-Detail Contract

Define the relationship between the master list and the detail surface.

Capture:

- master item type
- detail scope and depth
- selection source: URL, local state, or mixed
- preview data visible in the master list
- detail actions and primary next step
- state coverage: empty list, no selection, loading selection, unavailable record, and error

The contract should explain why both master and detail need to coexist.
