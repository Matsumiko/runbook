# Totals And Trust

Checkout UI must make money and trust legible.

Keep rules like these in mind:

- keep subtotal, discounts, fees, shipping, tax, and total easy to scan
- make the final payable amount visually clear
- keep policy, secure-payment, or billing trust cues calm and secondary
- keep the primary purchase CTA singular and specific
- avoid playful copy or decorative clutter near money and payment steps

Do not let coupons, upsells, or secondary links overshadow the main purchase action.
