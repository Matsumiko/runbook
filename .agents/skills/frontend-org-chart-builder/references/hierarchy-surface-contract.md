# Hierarchy Surface Contract

Define the org-chart surface contract before choosing layout geometry.

Capture:

- node type: employee, team, department, partner, account, or mixed
- root entry point and visible depth
- relationship type: direct report, dotted line, grouped department, assistant, or placeholder
- node card fields: name, title, team, status, avatar, count, location, or action menu
- node interactions: select, open detail panel, expand descendants, jump to route, or drag
- visibility rules for hidden descendants, missing managers, vacant roles, or redacted records
- state coverage: loading, empty, filtered, error, and selected-node states

Keep the contract honest about whether the hierarchy is a navigable product surface or a compact supporting visualization.
