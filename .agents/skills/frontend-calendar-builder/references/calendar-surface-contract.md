# Calendar Surface Contract

Use this reference to define the scheduling job before choosing a layout.

## Common calendar jobs

- Team or project schedule
- Booking or reservation view
- Editorial or publishing planner
- Agenda or operations schedule

## Contract questions

Clarify:

- month, week, day, or agenda as the primary view
- type of events or items shown
- overlap and all-day behavior
- navigation expectations
- filters or resource lanes
- empty or unavailable-period states

## Pattern boundary

- Use date-picker-builder for date-input controls.
- Use timeline-builder for chronological history when a planner grid is not needed.
