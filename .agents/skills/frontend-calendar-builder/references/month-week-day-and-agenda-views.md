# Month Week Day And Agenda Views

Choose the view model that best matches the user's planning task.

## Month view

- Best for broad planning and sparse density.
- Keep event chips concise and prioritize date context.

## Week or day view

- Best when time slots and overlap matter.
- Make time scale and overlapping event treatment readable.

## Agenda view

- Best for mobile fallback or dense chronological lists.
- Preserve date grouping and event priority without grid complexity.
